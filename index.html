    for the Business Owners

    Avail Home Loans at Attractive Interest Rates Based on Your Banking, Credit and Savings History


    Requirements for a Home Loan

    • You must have at least 3 ONLINE TRANSACTIONS for your business
    • Loan duration - Atmost (Maximum) 25 Years
    • Maximum age limit to avail loans - 70 Years
    • Minimum loan amount - 20 Lakhs
    • Maximum loan amount - 1 Crore
    • Your business registration must be at least 3 years old
    • Your loan will be sanctioned based on Banking Transactions, and not on your ITR
      • ! Most Important !

    Documentation Required for the Loan

    1. A Copy of your PAN Card
    2. A Copy of your Aadhar Card
    3. A Copy of your Address Proof (Electricity Bill, Sale Deed, etc)
    4. ITR Record for the Previous 3 Years (Including your Balance Sheets)
    5. e-Statement of your Bank Account (Savings / Current) for the previous 2 Years
    6. Business Registration Details (Must be At least 3 Years Old)
    7. 2 Photographs
    8. A Copy of your Property Papers
